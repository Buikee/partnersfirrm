Who We Are

Osunwa & Partners is a distinguished Nigerian law firm known for excellence, integrity, and advocacy. We were founded to close the gap between the privileged and the underprivileged in accessing justice.

Our team of experienced lawyers, including Senior Advocates of Nigeria (SAN), handle high-profile litigation, complex corporate advisory, and public interest cases. We are passionate about human rights, good governance, and community impact.

Our Foundational Principles

Our Mission

To make justice accessible and affordable while upholding the rule of law and empowering communities.

Our Vision

To be a leading force for justice, fairness, and innovation in legal practice across Africa.

Core Values

Integrity, Excellence, Empathy, and Service to humanity.
