The Role of Arbitration

A Better Way to Resolve Commercial Disputes

By Abdulmumeen Jimoh | February 5, 2025

Arbitration is emerging as a preferred alternative to litigation for resolving commercial disputes. It offers speed, confidentiality, and flexibility that traditional court processes often cannot match.

Advantages of Arbitration

  • Faster resolution compared to court cases
  • Confidential proceedings
  • Choice of neutral, expert arbitrators
  • International enforceability of awards
